# Hello World :earth_americas:
Hi, I'm Victor, most known as Carreira ✍️. I'm an earth scientist professor at Federal University of Acre ([UFAC](https://www.ufac.br/lab/gfisa)) with a passion for numerical modelling of solid-Earth problems, applied geophysics, and paleoclimate studies. My journey began in 1993 after I watched Jurassic Park 🦖 in a movie theater. I saw it again ... and again ... and again in an exponential fashion, and it sparked my curiosity about rocks and fossils. As a young school student, I worked at the [National Museum](https://pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/Museu_Nacional_(Rio_de_Janeiro)) :european_castle:, Brazil’s most incredible and amazing natural history museum of all time. It took some time before I started college, but I eventually earned my geology degree in 2012 at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro ([UFRJ](https://igeo.ufrj.br/)), focusing on economic studies of intracratonic sedimentary basins. I kept going, specialising in Geophysics at the National Observatory ([ON](https://www.gov.br/observatorio/pt-br)), where I completed my MSc in 2015 in applied geophysics :desktop_computer: and my PhD in 2021 in geophysical modelling using heuristic methods, inversion, ML, and AI :slot_machine:. For three years, I have been working as a postdoctoral researcher on the Organic Facies Modelling project Ressurgência, which won the Petrobras Inventor Award 2023 :trophy:, at Fluminense Federal University ([UFF](http://geosedi-uff.com.br/projeto-ressurgencia-3/)), resulting in two registered patents.
