# Hello World :earth_americas:
Hi, I'm Victor, most known as Carreira ✍️. I'm an earth scientist with a passion for numerical modelling of solid-Earth problems, applied geophysics, and paleoclimate studies. My journey began in 1993 after watching Jurassic Park 🦖 in the theatre. I saw it again ... and again ... and again in an exponential fashion, and it sparked my curiosity about rocks and fossils. As a young school student, I worked at the [National Museum](https://pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/Museu_Nacional_(Rio_de_Janeiro)), Brazil’s most incredible and amazing natural history museum of all time. It took some time before I started college, but I eventually earned my geology degree in 2012 at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ), focusing on economic studies of intracratonic sedimentary basins. I kept going, specialising in Geophysics at the National Observatory (ON), where I completed my MSc in 2015 and my PhD in 2021 in geophysical modelling using heuristic, inversion, ML and AI :slot_machine:.
